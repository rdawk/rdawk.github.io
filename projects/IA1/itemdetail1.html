The iPhone 2G


Wikipedia

The iPhone (retroactively referred to as the iPhone 2G or iPhone 1) is the first iPhone model and the first smartphone developed and marketed by Apple Inc.
After years of rumors and speculation, it was officially announced on January 9, 2007, and was released in the United States on June 29, 2007.
