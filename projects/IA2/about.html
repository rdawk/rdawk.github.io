Our Story

It all started with this record player. In 1968 my dad became obsessed with music while listening to vinyls on one of these. Years later, he decided to open a record shop to share his love of music with the world. Long story short, here we are today, open since 1977 and still selling records.

Although music has evolved, we still have the same plan as we have before, to share our love of music with the world.

Our Location

We originally started in a small shop in McKees Rocks, PA. After a couple of years, our original location was involved in a structure fire and was burnt to the ground.

We decided to rebuild in a better location and ended up moving our shop to downtown Pittsburgh, where we currently are located.
