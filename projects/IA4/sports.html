If there is one thing that people from Pittsburgh love, it's sports. Our city is founded on black and gold and it seems to run through almost every Pittsburgher's veins. Check out our sports teams, and maybe get some tickets below!

Pittsburgh Steelers

Head Coach: Mike Tomlin

Founded: 1933

Venue: Acrisure Stadium

Super Bowl Wins: 6

The Steelers were founded in 1933 by Arthur J. Rooney, and were orignally called the Pittsburgh Pirates. They changed their name to the Steelers in 1940. The 1970s was the best time for the Steelers, winning four Super Bowls and making playoffs 8 times.

Pittsburgh Pirates

General Manager: Derek Shelton

Founded: 1881

Venue: PNC Park

World Series Wins: 5

The Pirates were founded in 1881 by Denny McKnight and were orignally called the Pittsburgh Alleghenys. The name Pirates came along just 10 years later in 1891. The Pirates have five world series wins in 1909, 1925, 1960, 1971, and 1979.

Pittsburgh Penguins

Head Coach: Mike Sullivan

Founded: 1967

Venue: PPG Paints Arena

Stanley Cup Wins: 5

The Penguins were foundd in 1967 during the NHL's expansion. Unlike the Steelers and Pirates, they were orignally founded as the Penguins. They have five Stanley Cup wins in 1991, 1992, 2009, 2016, and 2017. The Penguins are tied for the most Stanley Cup championships among the non-original six teams.
